Coronavirus (COVID-19) Update

At JS Party Hire, we take safety very seriously. Actually, it is the most important thing!

Due to the awake of the Coronavirus (COVID-19), we have taken further measures to clean the castles and equipment with soap and antibacterial products (we do this on a normal day too!)

Since the Government's announcement of social distancing, we are no longer promoting parties or the like in the coming weeks. However, we will still help our valued customers keep their children entertained during this very stressful time. Having a bouncy castle for the children of only your home can get them busy for hours during the day, helping them release their energy and stay healthy.

JS Party Hire will deliver the Bouncy Castle in the safest possible method. Our Delivery driver will be wearing protective gear and after cleaning the castle thoroughly, we will set it up in your garden. We will ask all family members, especially the elderly and children, to go upstairs while the setup is taking place.

If you have any concerns or would like to discuss the arrangements then please give us a call on 020 8505 8115.
Can we please direct you to the guidance of the NHS services.
